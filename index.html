About Me

I am a data‑driven Senior Data Scientist and Epidemiologist with 13+ years of experience in biostatistics, real‑world data analytics, and machine‑learning applications in global health. Currently, I serve as a Health Scientist (Informatics & Data Science) at the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, where I design advanced analytics and AI solutions powering national and international infectious‑disease surveillance.

Across roles at USAID, CDC Nigeria, and leading NGOs, I have authored 30+ peer‑reviewed publications, led national HIV surveillance programs, and built AI‑driven tools that improve health‑care outcomes. My tool‑kit spans Python, R, SQL, AWS, and advanced ML techniques for causal inference, predictive modeling, and knowledge extraction.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity

This 8‑week live online certification from the GRAPH Network teaches no‑code AI tools for automating data analysis, content creation, knowledge management, and routine tasks. Through hands‑on workshops and a capstone project, participants learn to:

  • Automate document, spreadsheet, and data workflows
  • Organize literature and perform knowledge synthesis with AI
  • Develop custom chatbots and RAG systems
  • Create professional visuals and presentations with generative tools
  • Design AI‑powered automations to reclaim hours of work each week
